Award Program
In our research, we realize that a successful rewards program must be making a powerful connection between employee's performance and the reward. Those traditional "Employee of the Month" awards are meaningless because it often consists of a manager choosing an employee without regard to merit. Employees knew that if they stay long enough, they'll be chosen one day. That means, there's no motivation for improvement.

To make the awards meaningful and effectiveness, there are 5 rules must be follow:
- Decide EXACTLY what you want your employee to achieve, so they need to reach to earn a reward
- Choose rewards that will be valued by your people. Are they young, or maturity? If so, then the rewards may be a fashion mobile phone or money prize.
- Promote the criteria, benchmark and rewards regularly.
- Public recognition to all winners of award specifically.
- Deliver the rewards in a timely manner.

By rewarding or giving recognition to your excellent front-line staff, it can help motivate and reinforce them to do the best. Moreover, this service can certainly help to enhance staff morale.

To bottom-line staff, the rewards programs can also create a positive, challenging climate that assists companies in reaching their goals. In our research, companies that use rewards programs with mystery shopping programs can improve 1/3 faster than those who do not.
