●  Background

Mystery Shopper Service Company (MSS) is wholly owned by International Service Access Limited (ISA) which has been providing the Mystery Shopper Program since 2002. MSS is a cost effective tool to measure and evaluate the service standard of a company. Applying this service, company can get an insight in what areas they should be improved. This program is applicable to wide range of industries typically include food and beverage, retail, hotel and government branches, etc. Services coverage could be on-site shopping, telephone survey, competitive benchmark exercise. For the last few years, we have been serving a lot of world-wide well-known companies, conducted nearly 10,000 visits to varies retail outlets in different parts of Hong Kong.

In 2001, Mr. Anders Wong, General Manager, raised the idea of founding the MSSC due to a badly served experience:

"By Christmas in 2000, I remembered that I was having dinner in one of the high-class chain restaurant in Causeway Bay, Hong Kong. At that time, I experienced the extremely poor service during on served although the food quality deserved the good reputation. I was so embarrassed because it was the first time my oversea friend came to visit Hong Kong, and that meal was his first meal in Hong Kong so far. I knew that he already had a bad impression in Hong Kong which we can remedy.

At that night, I was bothered so much on the quality of service providing in Hong Kong. I understood that most of our service providers are able to provide excellent service, but they may not notice the importance of service quality to customers. I then started studying the oversea cases, I learned the Mystery Shopper Program that Hong Kong was yet to be popular at that time, no one using mystery shopping services knew exactly how to use it in the most effective, proactive and positive way. Hence, that was my first idea to establish Mystery Shopper Service Company in hong Kong. I have confident that our company can provide the best Mystery Shopper Service in Hong Kong region, and eventually we are able to enhance the overall quality of service in Hong Kong."
